Introduction

I’ve always been the consumer but never looked at shopping from the point of view from the business side of it. I don’t usually go to a store and wonder what the main selling item is or what do people need the most of. I found this data set from Kaggle to open my eyes and learn a little bit more about what we as the consumer usually buy and who’s the main one buying things.
